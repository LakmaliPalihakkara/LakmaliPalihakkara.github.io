About

Candice's House provides tailored activities, respite & accommodation for those living with a disability. Candice’s House has been created in partnership with community service provider Candice Care as a dedicated home of community services. Candice’s House offers tailored activities for those living with a disability and our ageing community, as well as accommodation for Respite, Short-Term (STA) and Medium-Term (MTA) purposes.

My Role

I have worked as an Android mobile application developer of Candice's House project, and I was mainly responsible for app coding, testing, debugging, documenting, and monitoring.
