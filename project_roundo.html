RoundO

About

The RoundO is a React-Native application that assists candidates in creating an accurate and practical resume. Users must provide their contact, education, work experience, and portfolio information to generate a link to a digital resume that may be sent straight to employers via the application as an email. This project is currently ongoing and will be released in August 2022.

My Role

I am working as a group member on RoundO project and I am mainly responsible for app designing, coding, testing, debugging, documenting, and monitoring.
